Welcome to DOCK Blaster, a public access service for structure-based ligand discovery. DOCK Blaster is a set of tools to help you find small molecules to test for activity against your biological target for which you have a structure.

DOCK Blaster is provided by the Irwin and Shoichet Laboratories in the Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF).

The current version of DOCK Blaster is unpublished. For now, to cite DOCK Blaster, please reference Irwin, Shoichet, Mysinger et al., "Automated docking screens: a feasibility study.", J. Med. Chem. 2009, 52(18), pp 5712-5720.

To use DOCK Blaster 18, you must create an account and log in.
